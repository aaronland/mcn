Ignite Reception

Our signature welcome event, MCN Ignite showcases a series of rapid-fire talks from creative thinkers in the museum field.Expect lots of energy with fresh, inspiring, and timely ideas from familiar faces, and emerging voices in the field and beyond.” Talks follow the “Ignite” format, in which presenters have five minutes and 20 slides (which advance automatically every 15 seconds) to enlighten, entertain, and inspire. Hear from several brave and visionary speakers sharing their experiences in a wide variety of disciplines:
