Interested in continuing an earlier conference discussion? Is there a topic you wish was covered during MCN 2023, but it wasn't? Are you looking for other attendees who might have the same questions as you? Welcome to Open Space Discussions (OSD)!

During this Opening Circle, you will generate session topics for discussions that will take place over the next two days.

Using topics generated during the Opening Circle and throughout the conference, we will designate a breakout space and a time for each discussion. Session attendees can participate in any of the dozens of topical discussions and may move from topic to topic as often as desired.
