Effective Search Engine Optimization for Artworks
At the Met Museum we doubled our traffic to our collection using basic, inexpensive SEO techniques that help guide our audience to the artworks they are searching for. In this session I'll show you how we did it, the tools we used, and also how we consistently monitor performance so that we are always connecting our collection to the people who are looking for it.
