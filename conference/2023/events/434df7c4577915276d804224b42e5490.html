Ready, Set, GO!: Launching a new staff Intranet

Log in to save this to your schedule, view media, leave feedback and see who's attending!

Tweet Share
How do you launch a new staff intranet? What if your old intranet platform is failing and it holds crucial legal, HR, technical, and safety information? And lunch menus! Plus lots of outdated materials. Meanwhile, experts and users across the organization need to, and want to, be involved, but everyone has other priorities. It is possible!

In 2020 Getty set out to update a 20-year-old staff intranet, successfully relaunching in April 2023. We moved from an outdated platform that only a few people could maintain, to a new, well organized, easily searchable, cloud-based site that people in departments across the organization can update themselves. And the new site promotes our inclusivity initiatives with features like a Spanish language portal, improved accessibility, and just plain better communication.

Keys to our success were:
• Involving stakeholders from across the organization
• Driving the project forward with a core team and ambitious deadlines
• Working with a vendor partner to provide expertise and perform heavy-lifting

We will share key elements of the project, with illustrations, quizzes and trivia questions:
• Surveying staff to develop requirements
• Involving leadership and staff
• Creating a working group of content owners throughout the organization
• Selecting and hiring an outside vendor
• Reorganizing the intranet to be intuitive for users
• Lifting and sifting content from the old site
• Following a phased approach to launch with a Minimum Viable Product (MVP)
• Training authors to update their own content
