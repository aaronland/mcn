Lessons in innovation: behind the scenes of tech success
Wednesday October 23, 2024 1:15pm - 1:35pm CDT
Adams Alumni Center, 2nd Floor - Bruckmiller Room
You're checked in!
Innovation fuels progress. This session will look at the “magic sauce” behind successful tech projects. We’ll look at what drives innovation: user needs, emerging technologies, fails, and a sprinkle of creative disruption. We’ll also think about what we can do to encourage a culture that embraces calculated risks, and celebrates the rewards of pushing boundaries, explore ideas for staying ahead of the curve by identifying the next cutting-edge advancements (surviving the current ones!) and fostering a psychologically safe environment where groundbreaking ideas can take off.
