Resilient by Design: How WCMA's MOSS App is Streamlining a Major Collection Move
Tuesday October 21, 2025 1:40pm - 2:00pm CDT
Herzog Building, 5th Floor - Skyline Room
You're checked in!
Faced with the challenge of relocating 15,000 collection objects in advance of a new building, the Williams College Museum of Art (WCMA) needed a location tracking solution that was fast, reliable, and easy to use. Working with Concrete Computing and Most Media, WCMA developed MOSS (Museum Object Scanning System), a lightweight web app built to streamline barcode-based inventory and moves, cutting task time in half while improving accuracy and staff coordination. This session offers a case study in rapid, resilient tool design—showing how targeted digital interventions can support institutional change, reduce friction in collaboration with IT departments, and maintain human-centered workflows at scale.
