DAM for Public Impact: Aligning Infrastructure with Public Mission
Tuesday October 21, 2025 2:15pm - 3:00pm CDT
Bazinet Lobby - Cinema
You're checked in!
Museums are under pressure to deliver public access, ethical AI use, and digital sustainability, but most DAM systems are still treated as internal storage. This session, led by the Canadian Museum for Human Rights and Orange Logic, will explore DAMs as cultural infrastructure, tools that shape access, trust, and storytelling. Attendees will leave with practical strategies to align their tech stack with mission-driven goals and public-facing needs.

Presented by Orange Logic, a community partner for this year's conference.
