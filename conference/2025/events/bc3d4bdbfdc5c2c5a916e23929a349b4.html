Emerging Tech in Art Museums: Three* Perspectives
Wednesday October 22, 2025 9:15am - 10:00am CDT
Herzog Building, 5th Floor - Skyline Room
You're checked in!
Can museums be hubs for innovation and experimentation? In art museums specifically, where should that innovation be centered: with artists, with public engagement, or somewhere in between? This panel will feature speakers from The Met and the Whitney, and explore three different takes (not “answers”) to those questions. Each presenter will briefly review their museum’s emerging technology strategy, where their role fits into the larger organization, and a case study representing their working model. Case studies will explore recent projects with AI, blockchain, and virtual worlds.

A panel discussion will follow, exploring technology in general (what we’re prioritizing, what we see as next, etc), and wrestling with what role—if any—museums should play in the larger tech landscape. The panel will also include space for audience questions and group discussion.
