Future-Proofing through Sustainability and Resilience
Tuesday October 21, 2025 11:15am - 12:00pm CDT
Bazinet Lobby - Cinema
You're checked in!
Following our joint session at MCN 2024, “One Collection to Rule Them All,” we're back to share the next chapter of our journey. Last year, we introduced a functional, unstyled prototype of a unified online collection discovery portal that sparked interest and meaningful conversation across the MCN community.

This year, we’ll walk through the evolution of that prototype into a fully launched beta MVP —delivered alongside a new institutional brand, a headless website, and a completed DAMS-CMS integration.

Our session will explore how we approached infrastructure sustainably: maintaining our existing CMS while redeveloping its public interface, integrating the DAMS, and building a unified collection experience. Together, these efforts have laid the foundation for future innovation. We're also hoping to be able to preview plans for our latest initiative: a “My Huntington” customer portal on our flagship website, a project that will centralize member account management, increase web-generated revenue, and improve customer experience.

Too often, long-term digital projects are only discussed in hindsight. We aim to share our experience as it continues to unfold—the wins, the challenges, and everything in between. Our hope is to foster a candid conversation about navigating these complex projects together.

Aligned with this year’s conference themes of resilience and resonance, we’ll reflect on how a strong client-vendor partnership enabled us to adapt, persevere, and deliver impactful results. We’ll share what we’ve learned about collaboration, communication, and sustaining momentum to achieve ambitious goals.
