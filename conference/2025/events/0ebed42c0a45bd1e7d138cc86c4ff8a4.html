Let’s Build The MCN Museum: Live-Building a New 3D Museum with MCN Community Assets
Tuesday October 21, 2025 11:15am - 12:00pm CDT
Barnes Building, 8th Floor - Garden Terrace Room
You're checked in!
The MiM platform lets non-technical users create 3D experiences of exquisite quality, and in the time it takes to create a PowerPoint. The platform lets collections open up access to their IP without losing control, and to make revenue along the way.

In this session, we will demonstrate the process of building an experience from start to finish with assets contributed by community members. This will showcase the Creator side of the platform, and we will be able to take questions and suggestions about the process.

There will be opportunities for members to experience the resultant MCN Museum with a dedicated session on Wednesday at 1pm at the MiM booth in the Community Partner Forum.

Presented by Museums in the Metaverse (MiM), a community partner for this year's conference.
