Vibe Coding & Questioning One's Raison d'Être: A Perspective on the Future of Creation
Wednesday October 22, 2025 1:15pm - 2:00pm CDT
Herzog Building, 3rd Floor - McGuire Theater
You're checked in!
This presentation explores how the rise of AI and rapid prototyping (dubbed "vibe coding") represents both a challenge and opportunity for experienced generalists. It critiques the current software development culture that prioritizes quick MVPs over sustainable, thoughtful solutions, while celebrating how those with diverse experiences across multiple disciplines are uniquely positioned to thrive in this new era.

The talk examines how technological shifts throughout history have reshaped creative fields, drawing parallels between the impact of the camera on painting and AI on software development. It argues that while anyone can now generate code or content quickly, the true value lies in perspective—understanding systems holistically, making meaningful connections between disciplines, and bringing hard-won experience to bear on complex problems.

The presentation concludes that we're entering a new Renaissance where breadth of experience, pattern recognition, and human judgment become the scarcest and most valuable resources—positioning seasoned generalists not as obsolete but as essential guides in an age of infinite generation. It's a call for reclaiming creativity, ownership and perspective in a world being rapidly transformed by AI.
